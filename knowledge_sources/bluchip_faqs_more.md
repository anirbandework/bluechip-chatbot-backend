There are 3 Tiers in the IndiGo BluChip Program viz. Blu 1, Blu 2 and Blu 3. Upon enrolment, an individual is enrolled into Blu 3 which is the entry level Tier. Members move up Tiers basis the achievement of the qualification criteria. Blu 1 is the highest Tier in the Program.

Membership to IndiGo BluChip Program is only for individuals and not for corporates or entities. Individuals who are part of corporates or entities can enrol into IndiGo BluChip Program in their individual capacity.

### What can a Member redeem IndiGo BluChips for?

IndiGo BluChips can be redeemed only on IndiGo Flights (excluding IndiGo Marketed and Codeshare Partner operated flight). IndiGo BluChips cannot be redeemed for any 6E Add-ons, taxes, levies, surcharges, fees, etc. on their IndiGo Flights.

Step 3: Member can choose the preferred flight from all the flight options displayed in IndiGo BluChips and redeem for the flight either 100% in form of IndiGo BluChips or part IndiGo BluChips and part in Cash. Step 4: Members will have to pay for other levies, taxes, ancillary purchases, etc. in form of currency through any form of payment accepted on IndiGo Website/ IndiGo App.

Only IndiGo marketed and operated flights are eligible for redemptions. IndiGo marketed; Codeshare Partner operated flights are not eligible for redemption. Redemption can be done only on one-way basis for IndiGo flights. Multi-city bookings, special fares for senior citizens and other categories are not available for IndiGo BluChips redemption. If there is an infant travelling along with the Member, then applicable infant fare will have to paid by the Member in the form and manner as prescribed by IndiGo.

No, there are no blackout dates for redemption bookings. Member can redeem against flights throughout the year, anytime, on any sector until the last available seat.

No, IndiGo BluChips can only be earned for revenue flights booked through cash, card, UPI or other forms of monetary payments.

No, IndiGo BluChips do not have any monetary value and cannot be exchanged for cash. IndiGo BluChips can only be used to book IndiGo flights.

### If a Member has made a redemption booking using IndiGo BluChips + Cash will the Member earn IndiGo BluChips for cash payment done?

No, IndiGo BluChips cannot be earned for redemption bookings made using IndiGo BluChips or IndiGo BluChips and cash.

### Can a Member transfer his/her IndiGo BluChips to someone else?

No, transfer of IndiGo BluChips is not permitted as per the Terms and Conditions of the IndiGo BluChip Program.

Redemption fee, convenience fee, levies and statutory taxes are applicable per passenger per sector and will be charged separately in applicable currency. Redemption fee and convenience fee are non-refundable.

## Cancellations and Modifications for Redemption Bookings (Full Redemption and Partial Redemption)

### How can a Member cancel their redemption booking?

### Can a Member modify his/her redemption booking?

No, redemption bookings cannot be modified. Members must cancel and rebook their tickets. For example, if a member has booked a ticket for travel on 1st January 2025 from Delhi to Jaipur in Economy Class by redeeming 5,000 IndiGo BluChips, and later wishes to make any of the following changes: Date change Destination change Cabin class change Such modifications are not allowed, as the booking was paid for using IndiGo BluChips redemption. The member must cancel the existing ticket and make a new booking. Refunds will be processed as per the applicable cancellation policy.

## Program Tiers and Tier Benefits

### What are the IndiGo BluChip Tiers?

The IndiGo BluChip program has three Tiers – Blu 1, Blu 2, Blu 3. Members begin at the Blu 3 Tier and progress to higher Tiers of Blu 2 and Blu 1. Eligibility criteria for upgrade: Members need to spend a minimum of INR 100,000 and complete 04 IndiGo flights to upgrade or retain Blu 2 Tier Members need to spend a minimum of INR 200,000 and complete 08 IndiGo flights to upgrade or retain Blu 1 Tier Tier validity: Blu 1 and Blu 2 Tiers are valid for a period of 12 months. Members need to qualify for retaining or upgrading their Tiers basis the above-mentioned Tier eligibility criteria. Blu 3 Tier has lifetime validity, however, is reviewed annually for the purpose of assignment of Tier Benefits. Tier evaluation: Tiers are evaluated for upgrade daily to check the previous 365 days period for the minimum spends and flights criteria. Tiers are evaluated for retention/downgrade once on the Tier’s anniversary to check the previous 365 days period for the minimum spends and flights criteria.

### What flights and spends qualify for Tier evaluation?

Amount of fare paid towards the base fare and fuel surcharge component on revenue tickets on flights mentioned below qualify for Tier evaluation: IndiGo marketed and operated flights. IndiGo marketed and Codeshare Partner operated flights. Flights undertaken through redemption of IndiGo BluChips either in full or in part IndiGo BluChips and part cash do not qualify for Tier evaluation.

### Can the Member use their Tier benefits for co-passengers?

Tier Benefits can be used by Members for self and/or Nominees only. Other co-passengers are not eligible to use Tier benefits.

### Can Tier Benefits be used on flights booked through redemption of IndiGo BluChips?

Tier Benefits viz. 6E Prime and discounts on select 6E Add-ons can be used on both revenue and redemption flights of Members and/or Nominees.

### Can Tier Benefits be used on IndiGo marketed; Codeshare Partner operated flights?

No. Tier Benefits cannot be used on IndiGo marketed; Codeshare Partner operated flights.

### What bookings qualify for use of Tier Benefits?

Bookings on IndiGo marketed and operated flights booked only through IndiGo Website/ IndiGo Mobile App qualify for use of Tier Benefits like 6E Prime or discount on select 6E Add-ons. Corporate bookings, bookings made through OTAs, travel agents, agent/SME portal on www.goindigo.in or third-party website or through any other channels do not qualify for use of Tier Benefits of 6E Prime or discounts on select 6E Add-ons. Group bookings or charter bookings also do not qualify for use of Tier Benefits of 6E Prime or discounts on select 6E Add-ons.

### Can the Member use Tier Benefits on bookings after the payment has been made?

IndiGo BluChip members can avail Tier benefits after payment only on Tier-discounted services such as Seat Select, Excess Baggage, and Fast Forward.

### Will Members earn Tier Bonus IndiGo BluChips for bookings made through channels other than IndiGo Website/ IndiGo Mobile App?

Yes. Members will earn Tier Bonus IndiGo BluChips over and above the Base IndiGo BluChips irrespective of their channel of booking. Bookings made through OTAs, travel agents, agent/SME portal on www.goindigo.in or third-party website or through any other channels qualify for earning of Tier Bonus IndiGo BluChips.

### How will the Member know that Tier Benefits have been used in the booking?

Tier Benefits are updated in the booking and appear on the e-ticket itinerary, or the boarding pass issued to the Member. Following code that will appear in your boarding pass and e-ticket if you have selected the Tier Benefit while booking on IndiGo Website or IndiGo Mobile App PRIM FFWD

### As a Blu 1 or Blu 2 Member can they access priority check-in or priority boarding through the Fast Forward desk?

Only if the booking has a 6E Prime or a Fast Forward Add-on updated in the booking can a Member use the facilities offered through Fast Forward. Members are advised to check their bookings for presence of Fast Forward Add-on for availing the facilities.

### Can a Member use only part of the features of 6E Prime?

6E Prime is a combo product Add-on offered by IndiGo. A 6E Prime product includes Seat Select, Snack combo and Fast Forward Service. Members cannot use only part of the product offering of 6E Prime. Once 6E Prime benefit is used, all features of the product are applied to the booking. Currently Seat Select, Snack Combo and Fast Forward Service is offered as a combo product. Services offered in the combo product can be updated from time to time by IndiGo and will be updated on IndiGo Website and Mobile App.

### What is the validity of Tier benefits?

Tier Benefits remain valid until the expiry date of the Member's current Tier. These benefits can be applied to flights where booking and travel is within the validity period of the Tier. If a Member's Tier is upgraded or downgraded during this period, the Tier Benefits associated with the previous Tier will expire, and the Member will receive benefits according to their new Tier. Due to change in the Tier of the Member especially when the Member has been downgraded to a Tier where certain Tier Benefits are not available e.g. 6E Prime, then in such instances if the Member makes any changes to their bookings, applicable charges for such benefits will be payable by the Member. Furthermore, other Tier Benefits as applicable as per the revised Tier will apply in case of purchase of an eligible ancillary at the time of making the purchase of the ancillary and not as per the Tier at the time when the booking was made.

### What will happen to Member’s Tier Benefits if they cancel a booking?

Tier Benefits (6E Prime) will be re-deposited to the Member’s Account if the booking is cancelled within the validity period of the Tier Benefit. If the Member has utilized any 6E Prime benefit the same will be re-deposited to the Member’s Account within 24 hours of cancellation of the booking with such re-deposited Tier Benefit bearing its original validity. Example: Booking date: 14 October 2024 Travel date: 10 November 2024 6E Prime Tier Benefit utilized: Yes. 6E Prime validity: 31 December 2024 If Member cancels the booking any time before the travel date, the voucher will be re-deposited with the original validity of 31 December 2024.   If the validity of the said Tier Benefit has expired, then the Tier Benefit will not be reinstated to the IndiGo BluChip Account.

### What will happen to my Tier Benefit if I modify a booking?

If the Member has utilized any 6E Prime Tier Benefits in the original booking, these will be carried forward to the modified booking of the Member if the modified date is within the validity period of the Tier Benefit utilized. Example 1: Booking date: 14 October 2024 Travel date: 10 November 2024 6E Prime Tier Benefit utilized: Yes. 6E Prime validity: 31 December 2024 Modified date of travel: 15 December 2024. 6E Prime Tier Benefit will be carried forward for the modified booking as the modified date of travel is within the Tier Benefit’s validity date.   Example 2: Booking date: 14 October 2024 Travel date: 10 November 2024 6E Prime Tier Benefit utilized: Yes. 6E Prime Tier Benefit validity: 15 November 2024 Booking modification booking date: 4 November 2024 Revised travel date: 05 December 2024 The complimentary 6E Prime Tier Benefit applied to the original booking will be carried forward to the modified booking.  However, the Member will need to pay any differential amount for such 6E Add-on to proceed with changes to the booking. The Tier Benefit will be forfeited as it will be past its validity date.

### What happens to the Member’s cobrand card ticket voucher used in the booking in case the Member cancels the flight?

For bookings where co-brand card ticket voucher has been used, the same will not be re-deposited if the flight has been cancelled.

### Can the Member use Tier Benefits on select 6E Add-ons on codeshare flights?

Tier Benefits are only applicable on IndiGo marketed and operated and not on IndiGo marketed, Codeshare Partner operated flights.

## Tier Upgrade, Downgrade and Retention

### What are the upgrade requirements from Blu 3 to Blu 2 Tier?

Member must spend a minimum of INR 100,000 and complete a minimum of 04 IndiGo flights in a continuous period of preceding 365 days to move from Blu 3 to Blu 2 Tier. Each day, Members are reviewed for Tier upgrades based on their activities in a continuous period of preceding 365 days.

### What are the upgrade requirements from Blu 2 to Blu 1 Tier?

Member must spend a minimum INR 200,000 and complete a minimum of 08 IndiGo flights in a continuous period of preceding 365 days to move from Blu 2 to Blu 1 Tier. Each day, Members are reviewed for Tier upgrades based on their activities in a continuous period of preceding 365 days.

### What will happen to the Members Tier Benefits of the previous Tier once the Member upgrades, or downgrades to a different Tier or retains the existing Tier?

Upon upgrade or downgrade of the Tier, Tier Benefits applicable for the revised Tier will be applied to the Member’s IndiGo BluChip Account with a revised Tier Benefit validity. All Tier Benefits associated to the previous Tier will expire. Similarly in case of retention of the same Tier, a fresh set of Tier Benefits will be issued and unutilized Tier Benefits will expire.

### How will the Member know when they have been upgraded to the next Tier level?

Members will receive a Tier upgrade notification via email sent on their registered email ID. Alternatively, Members can check their Tier status in the Dashboard section of their IndiGo BluChip Account.

### What are the retention requirements for Blu 3 Tier?

Blu 3 Tier is valid for lifetime and there is no expiry. Hence there are no retention criteria.

### What will happen if the Member is unable to meet the Tier retention requirements?

If a member does not meet the Tier retention requirements, they will be downgraded to the next lower Tier at the end of the 365-day Tier Review Period. Tier Benefits associated with the downgraded Tier will be awarded to the Member, while the previous Tier’s Benefits will expire.

### Is the Member’s redemption booking eligible for Tier review?

No, redemption bookings made using IndiGo BluChips or IndiGo BluChips + Cash will not be eligible for Tier Review.

### What will happen to the Member’s co-brand card benefits in case of Tier change?

There will be no impact on co-brand benefits (Co brand vouchers). Co-brand benefits have a different expiry date from that of Member’s Tier and Tier Benefit validity date.

## Expiry of IndiGo BluChips

### Do the IndiGo BluChips expire?

IndiGo BluChips are valid for a period of 24 months from the date of credit in Members’ account. However, IndiGo BluChips will be extended for another calendar month if the Member does an eligible Accrual or a Redemption activity on IndiGo or any Program Partner. Any IndiGo BluChips credited retrospectively to the Members’ Account after the expiry of IndiGo BluChips will not result in reinstatement of expired IndiGo BluChips.

### Do IndiGo BluChips earned on Program Partners have a different expiry than IndiGo BluChips earned for IndiGo flights?

IndiGo BluChips earned on Program Partners have the same expiry as IndiGo BluChips earned on IndiGo flights unless explicitly stated otherwise as per the Terms and Conditions.
